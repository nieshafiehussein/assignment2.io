Sparring

Passion for martial arts

In Sparring, two pesilat will face off against each other, to score as many points as possible. A punch will constituent 1 point, a kick 2 points and making the opponent fall will give 3 points. As safety is paramount in any sports competition, a vest is required for the match to commence and no parts above the chest and below the hip area should be attacked.
